Taking Narvik to the stars

A Narvik based technical student organization dedicated to space exploration, satellite technology, and aerospace innovation

About Polarix UIT

From lecture notes to Low Earth Orbit - Polarix UIT is a student led organization at UIT The Arctic University of Norway, dedicated to providing students with hands-on experience in the world of aerospace engineering.

Student Focused

The organization is entirely student-led, providing a platform for young engineers to take initiative and lead projects. We believe in empowering students to develop leadership skills while gaining practical experience in aerospace engineering.

Unique Opportunity

UiT The Arctic University of Norway offers bachelor’s and master’s programs in satellite and aerospace engineering. We aim to enrich students’ academic journeys through hands-on projects, real-world collaborations, and innovative challenges that prepare them for the space industry.

Guided by Expertise

With over 20 years of experience in space technology and research at UiT Narvik, our organization is guided by a strong legacy of expertise. We are committed to building on this foundation by involving students in relevant projects and encouraging a culture of innovation.

Our Projects

SERPENT

Designing and building a 2U CubeSat prototype for Suborbital flight.

PLACEHOLDER

Building a network of student-operated telescopes for astronomical observations and data collection.

RALLAR

Anual onboarding program for new members, focused on first year students. Aimed to give them the necessary technical and systems engineering skills.
