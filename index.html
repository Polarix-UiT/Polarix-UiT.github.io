About Our Mission

About Polarix UIT

Polarix UIT is a student led organization at UIT The Norwegian Arctic University, dedicated to providing students with hands-on experiance in the world of aerospace engineering.

Student Focused

The organization is entirely student-led, providing a platform for young engineers to take initiative and lead projects.

Unique opportunity

UiT The Arctic University of Norway offers bachelor and master's programs in aerospace engineering, we aim to provide students with practical experience through hands-on projects and collaborations.

Rich history

UIT Narvik has more than 20 years of experiance with space technology and research. We aim to build on this legacy by engaging students in innovative projects and collaborations.

Our Projects

SERPENT

Designing and building a 2U CubeSat prototype for Suborbital flight.

🔭 Observatory Network PLACEHOLDER

Building a network of student-operated telescopes for astronomical observations and data collection.

RALLAR

Anual onboarding program for new members, focused on first year students. Aimed to give them the necessary technical and systems engineering skills.

🚀 Model Rockets

Designing and launching model rockets to understand propulsion and atmospheric physics.

🛸 CubeSat Development

Working on our own CubeSat mission for educational and research purposes.

🌟 Outreach Programs

Organizing workshops and events to inspire the next generation of space enthusiasts.
