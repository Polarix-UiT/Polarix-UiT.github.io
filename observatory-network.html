Project Timeline

January 2025

Phase 1: Foundation

Established first telescope station on campus and developed remote control software. Successfully conducted initial test observations and data collection protocols.

March 2025

Phase 2: Network Expansion

Connected with 5 partner universities and established standardized communication protocols. Began collaborative observation sessions.

June 2025

Phase 3: Data Integration

Launched centralized data repository and analysis platform. Students can now access and analyze data from all network telescopes.

September 2025

Phase 4: Research Projects

Initiated first major collaborative research project: long-term monitoring of variable stars in the Andromeda galaxy.

December 2025

Future: Full Network

Goal to have 20+ telescopes online with advanced AI-assisted observation scheduling and automated data processing.
